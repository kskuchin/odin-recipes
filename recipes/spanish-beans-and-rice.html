Spanish Beans and Rice

Description

Spanish beans and rice dish as published by Dishing Out Health, with some edits based on how I make it at home.

From the site:

"Spanish Rice and Beans uses pantry staples to create a nutritious, flavorful one pot meal. Vegetarian, gluten free, and so simple to make, this rice and beans recipe will become a staple.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for added heat)
  • 1 (14.5-oz.) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 2 (15.5-oz.) cans kidney beans
  • ~2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Wash 1 cup of white rice, add water up to 2 cup mark, and prepare rice in rice cooker (night before if possible).
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium.
  3. Add onion; cook 5 minutes, until softened.
  4. Add garlic, paprika, salt, chili powder, oregano, black pepper, and cayenne; cook 2 minutes, stirring often, until aromatic.
  5. Stir in tomatoes and beans, then simmer until slightly thickened.
  6. Stir in chopped parsley, then season further with salt and pepper, to taste.
